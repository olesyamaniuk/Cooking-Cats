Cooking Cats

Master the Art of Cooking with Your Feline Friends!

  • Join the adorable Cooking Cats on a delightful culinary adventure!

  • Help the mischievous cats prepare scrumptious dishes in this fun and fast-paced puzzle game.

  • Match ingredients, cook up tasty meals, and serve hungry customers as you unlock new cats, kitchens, and exciting challenges.

  • With each level, the stakes get higher — can you handle the heat in the kitchen?

  • Cooking Cats offers a blend of cooking creativity, strategy, and puzzles perfect for food lovers and puzzle fans alike.

Benefits

  • Engaging Gameplay

    Fun and easy-to-learn puzzle mechanics with cooking-themed twists.

  • Expansive Content

    Over 1200 levels with new recipes, customers, and challenges added regularly.

  • Social Features

    Team up with friends, compete in cooking tournaments, and rise through the leaderboards.

  • Daily Rewards

    Get bonuses for logging in every day and completing special cooking challenges.

About This Game

  • Cooking Cats is developed by Starflow Path, a studio dedicated to creating engaging and family-friendly mobile games.
  • Players will take on the role of these lovable felines, preparing meals for customers and exploring new kitchens, all while managing time and ingredients efficiently.
  • With its unique puzzle mechanics, charming cat characters, and vibrant art style, Cooking Cats offers a delightful blend of puzzle-solving and cooking.
  • Cooking Cats provides endless fun, unique challenges, and a playful storyline that will keep players entertained for hours.

How to Play

  • Match Ingredients

    Tap to match three or more of the same ingredients to prepare your dishes and serve your customers.

  • Unlock Power-Ups

    Use special boosters like the "Meow Mixer," "Whisking Power," and "Chef’s Hat" to complete tough levels.

  • Complete Cooking Challenges

    Solve fun objectives such as preparing special orders, collecting ingredients, and serving the right meals.

  • Upgrade Your Kitchen

    Unlock new kitchens, special equipment, and upgrade your feline chefs for faster, more efficient cooking.

  • Participate in Events

    Join time-limited events like the "Purrfect Feast" to win exclusive rewards and bonuses.

FAQ

  • How do I start playing?
    Download the game from Google Play, complete the tutorial, and start cooking.
  • Are there in-app purchases?
    Yes, optional purchases for boosters, extra lives, and premium ingredients.
  • Can I play offline?
    Yes, Cooking Cats supports offline play with uninterrupted gameplay.
  • What are the team features?
    Form teams to collaborate, compete in cooking tournaments, and share rewards.
  • How do power-ups work?
    Boosters like Meow Mixer and Whisking Power help clear tough puzzles and make the game more fun.
  • How many levels are there?
    Over 1200 levels, with fresh challenges and new features added regularly.
  • What is the "Purrfect Feast"?
    A limited-time event where you can cook special dishes to win grand prizes.
  • Who is the developer?
    Cooking Cats is developed by Starflow Path, known for creating fun and family-friendly puzzle games.
